The Best Movies According to Chisimdiri Innocent

My top 3 movies of all time

Movie 1: The Train

I love 'The Train' for its powerful storytelling, which reminds us that our past doesn't define our future. The film's message of hope and redemption resonates deeply.

Movie 2: Abejoye

Abejoye's impactful portrayal of salvation and transformation inspires me. The film shows that no matter our struggles, God's love and mercy are always within reach.

Movie 3: Higher Calling

Higher calling challenges me to deepen my relationship with God. The film's thought-provoking themes and authentic characters encourage viewers to prioritize their spiritual journey.
